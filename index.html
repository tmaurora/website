VISION

Our vision is to commercialize the rover and develop into a product ready to move on Mars.

MISSION

Our mission is to develop a new gen rover which is competitive on par with other successful rovers worldwide.

ABOUT US

We are a group of 32 under graduate students from different branches of engineering from PSG Institute of Technology And Applied Research who tirelessly work to build a rover which is capable of running in Martian terrains and our team was formed on March 2019.

Currently the team is divided into 10 subsystems each consisting of highly dedicated students.

Each subsystem has a highly talented and skilled professors for mentoring the students throughout the mission

WHAT WE DO

Our team is currently working to participate in International Rover Challenge, an international robotics competition which engage the students to built next-generation Mars Rovers and compete in Mars stimulated situation conducted by Mars Society of South-Asia.

Most of the South-Asian students participate in this competition to show their talents.

Our team members are putting their best to rank high in the competitions we participate.

Other than just creating rover, our team learned a lot and improved well in each stage and each competition.
